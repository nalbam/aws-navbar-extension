Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2025

AWS Colorful Navbar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Overview

AWS Colorful Navbar is a browser extension that enhances the AWS Console experience by adding visual region indicators. This extension operates entirely locally on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cookies are used
  • No third-party services are integrated

Local Storage

The extension stores your preferences locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). This includes:

  • Background toggle — Enable/disable navbar color
  • Flag toggle — Enable/disable region flag display
  • Custom colors — Your personalized gradient colors
  • Theme preference — Dark or light mode setting

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • activeTab — To modify the appearance of AWS Console pages
  • storage — To save your preferences locally
  • Host permission — Limited to *.console.aws.amazon.com only

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. All functionality is self-contained.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Open Source

This extension is completely open source. You can review the source code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices yourself.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
